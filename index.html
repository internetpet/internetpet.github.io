Summer-Winter 2023

HM  kingpiehi (12.16) Fox



While he spent the first half of 2023 nursing an injury from a biking accident, the goodnight gamer found himself back to his previous level once recovered. He spent the latter half of 2023 mostly placing his seed at events, picking up a win on Quest at Kings of Hali 6 and also playing his matches in pool instead of melee.

HM  Petra - (10.82) - Fox

AJC defines the "Rising Star" tagline and has got there through sheer dedication to this game. The second most active Atlantic Canada player had over 300 sets under his belt in 2021 and doesn't stop there. He's very active offline, helping organize every in person SSBM event in Prince Edward Island for months now, and taking 1st place at three of them.

In the past few months is when he's got his best wins; on OtES, Glumbo and Asuka. As a mainstay in the 5th-7th range of Slippi Sauce, to me, it feels like it's not if he will figure out what he needs to do to level up, but when?

✏️ : Petra

No. 10 brad nasty - (10.22) - Captain Falcon, Fox, Ice Climbers

Have to play a Falcon next, I'm good at that matchup, it should be easy. Not sure who they are though. The tag is NS Melee Museum.

Too bad you might not get a chance to play their Falcon at all. Brad, local multi-character expert will pull out any character to secure the win. This penchant helped Brad often during this season as they boasted a 3–1 record against Petra in the Fox ditto and traded sets with Slab Crab using Falcon. Any bets on what characters they pick up next season?

No. 9 W3EM - (8.04) - Peach, Jigglypuff

One of the players that nearly didn't make it on this PR finds himself uniquely low as a previous top 5 player in the province. A loss to Slab Crab at once of his two events but a good performance at Kings of Hali puts him in a weird spot.

No. 8 Slab Crab - (7.50167) - Dr. Mario

While on the outside this seems like a purely negative result for Slab Crab as she moves 2 spots down on the PR, it is only thanks to her dedication to the game that she's made everyone around her better. Whether it is an entire tournament series, a specific character or player matchup, she will devote as much energy as possible into pushing ahead. If you upset her in bracket, you should be weary for the next time you meet.

No. 7 Dusty - (7.5) - Marth

Despite the little data we have for Dusty it's clear he belongs in this area of the PR. Even though he has close calls against some players below him, he eventually prevails or bounces back in the next bracket. During this season he traded sets with gwif, made it to Day 3 at Kings of Hali 6 and fended off loose cannons SuperHamster and Quest.



No. 6 gwif - (6.9) - Dr. Mario, Luigi

The first member of the mid-level trio that is himself, Dusty and Slab Crab. This past season, gwif found himself getting the better of the other two for the first time, boasting a 3–1 record against Slab Crab and 1–1 record with Dusty. Will gwif rest on his laurels or continue to push ahead in a time where he often finds himself in top 6 of a bracket with ease, facing down titans NSFrog and Inky?

No. 5 glumbo (4.82) - Fox

Similar to many players in this region, glumbo has taken a step back from competitive melee in the latter half of 2023. Instead of appearing at tournaments he's been focusing his efforts into his alternative-pop project 'chasing luma' for which he's released 3 singles in that time. Despite this he's put up exciting out-of-practice performances at GOML 2023 and Kings of Hali 6 where he got 97th and 13th respectively.

No. 4 K - (3.96) - Sheik

An enigmatic player. Every time he appears, he's inexplicably stronger. No one knows how much he practices or how this is possible. In his most recent outings he's shown that he has ascended to the next level, making quick work of past rival clomz0 and double eliminating NSFrog at an event just a few weeks past PR cutoff. Will he go back into hiding or continue surprise?

No. 3 LilMitts - (2.3) - Jigglypuff

It feels unfair to give LilMitts the 3rd place slot as he's been dominant and the only other player to take a set off Inky, but his Kings of Hali 6 run left something to be desired. He's knocking on the door in-region, but he'll have to grow even more to take the 2nd place spot that has been occupied by GOATs of Nova Scotia Melee.



No. 2 NSFrog - (2.28) - Captain Falcon, Yoshi

The perinnial second place master has his work cut out for him as his comtemporaries LilMitts and K have both powered up while NSFrog has put melee on the back burner, feeling less passion than ever. Here's to hoping he can sort everything out, find the time and passion for Melee. lets go frog woooo



No. 1 Inky - (1.0) - Sheik, Fox, Yoshi

Largely undefeatable unless he is going Fox or his newly minted Yoshi. Even when his closest opponents NSFrog and LilMitts take sets off his secondaries and force him to pick Sheik, he answers back swiftly.

In 2023 his out of region results finally lined up with what we all know he's possible of. Finding himself in Top 64 of Collision, GOML, Shine, The Big House and the pre-locals inbetween, Inky picked up wins on Jah' Ridin, Captain Smuckers, Grab, Nickemwit, Slowking and Swift.
